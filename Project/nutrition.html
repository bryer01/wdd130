Where Working Out And Nutrition Go Hand In Hand

Fruits Veggies Meats

When focusing on your nutrition for working out you must pick things that are high in fat and protein. That is key to keeping your body in shape. Your body uses its stored energy sources during a workout. After you exercise, you need to restore those nutrients as soon as possible. Research suggests that eating foods high in protein after your workout (within 15 minutes), provides essential amino acids that build and repair muscles. There are several rules to follow for nutrition. Rule # 1: Pay attention, Rule # 2: Fuel up (even if your goal is to lose weight), Rule # 3: Love carbs (you need them), Rule # 4: Rebuild with protein, Rule # 5: Don't ignore fats, Rule # 6: Know what you need pre-workout, Rule # 7: Remember the post-workout 15.
